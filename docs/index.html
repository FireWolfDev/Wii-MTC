Wii-MTC

An Add-on for Blender to make sure your models can fit your Wii game requirements.

How to install

1. Download wii-mtc.py

2. Open Blender 4.x.x

3. Go to Preferences > Add-ons

4. Click on Install from hard disk

5. Select wii-mtc.py

6. Toggle the add-on

How to use it

Go to the right panel (by pressing N), then go to Triangles > Toggle Enable Object coloration Then select your game

The objects who can fit the requirements will be in Blue and those who can't fit will be in Red.

Enjoy ! :)

Warnings

Max triangles are an approximation and some of your models may not work even under this limit. If this is the case, please open an Issue mentionning the game, and the working triangles count.
